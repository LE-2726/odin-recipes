Banana Bread

Description

Who doesn't love warm, banan filled banana bread fresh from the oven? This recipe is sure to please your family and friends. (If you can manage to leave some for them!) All you need are basic baking ingredients and some overripe bananas. Dig in!


Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1tsp baking soda
  • 1/4tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5 in loaf pan
  • In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
  • Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
